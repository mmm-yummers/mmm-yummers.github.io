Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

~ Ingredients ~

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 4 slices of American cheese
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Butter, for spreading

~ Instructions ~

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook the bacon until crispy.
  3. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  5. Place 2 slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
  6. Add a slice of cheese and a few tomato slices on top of each slice of bread.
  7. Top with the cooked bacon and remaining slices of cheese.
  8. Place the remaining slices of bread on top, butter side up.
  9. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  10. Remove from skillet and cut in half before serving.
